Why Choose AUBazaar?

Built specifically for the Africa University community

Verified Community

Only Africa University email addresses (@africau.edu) are allowed, ensuring a trusted campus-only marketplace.

Save Money

Buy used textbooks, electronics, and essentials at a fraction of the retail price from fellow students.

Eco-Friendly

Promote sustainability by giving items a second life instead of throwing them away. Reduce campus waste.

Easy Communication

Built-in messaging system to negotiate prices and arrange safe on-campus meetups easily.
