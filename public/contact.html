🏫 About AU Bazaar

Welcome to AU Bazaar, the official campus marketplace for Africa University! We're a student-powered platform designed to make buying and selling within the AU community easy, safe, and convenient.


Whether you're looking to sell your old textbooks, buy electronics, or find dorm essentials, AU Bazaar connects you with fellow students and staff. We verify all users have valid Africa University email addresses to ensure a trusted marketplace environment.


Our Mission: To create a sustainable circular economy within Africa University where students can save money on essentials and earn extra income from items they no longer need.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use AU Bazaar?

AU Bazaar is exclusively for Africa University students, staff, and faculty. You must have a valid @africau.edu or @students.africau.edu email address to register.

Is it free to use?

Yes! AU Bazaar is completely free for all AU community members. There are no listing fees or commissions.

How do I contact a seller?

Click on any listing and use the "Message Seller" button to start a conversation. All messaging happens through our secure in-app messaging system.

How do I list an item?

Click "Sell" in the navigation, fill in the details about your item, add photos, and publish. It's that simple!

What can I sell?

You can sell textbooks, electronics, clothing, dorm essentials, sports equipment, services, food, and more. Just no furniture - students live in dorms!

How do I report a problem?

Contact us at support@aubazaar.edu or use the Report button on any listing if you see something inappropriate.
